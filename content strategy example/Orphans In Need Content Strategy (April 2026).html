Strategy overview

Overview

A breakdown of the key areas this strategy addresses and how they connect.

Opportunity

There is a significant seasonal search opportunity around Qurbani and Eid al-Adha, with high-volume terms like "when is eid al adha 2026" (27,100 searches/mo) and "qurbani eid 2026" (3,600 searches/mo) that Orphans In Need can capture with the right content in place ahead of the peak period.

Plan structure

This strategy covers optimisations to 35 existing pages, 27 new landing pages, 9 supporting blog posts, and targeted link building across 5 key pages — all designed to strengthen Orphans In Need's organic visibility for Qurbani and Eid-related searches in the lead-up to Dhul Hijjah 2026.

Priority period

The immediate priority should be the Qurbani Guide mega page and the Eid al-Adha blog post, as these target the highest-volume keywords (27,100 and 14,800 searches/mo respectively) and need to be indexed and gaining authority well before the seasonal spike.

Keyword scope

Across all content pieces, this strategy targets over 150 keywords spanning Qurbani donations, Eid al-Adha traditions, Islamic prayer, and charitable giving — covering both high-volume seasonal terms and longer-tail informational queries that build topical authority year-round.
