Overview

What this strategy covers

This plan targets the keyword opportunities with the highest relevance and search volume for Orphans In Need, focusing on Qurbani, Dhul Hijjah, and evergreen Islamic content that drives year-round donor traffic.

The opportunity

The site already ranks for a large number of Qurbani and seasonal Islamic terms. With targeted content refreshes and new pages, we will push those rankings higher and capture a significant volume of high-intent searches in the run-up to Eid al-Adha 2026.

How the plan is structured

Content is split into four types: existing pages to optimise, new donation-focused landing pages, blog content targeting informational queries, and a link building outreach plan targeting the site's key conversion pages.

Priority period

Dhul Hijjah 2026 begins around late May. All Qurbani-related content and optimisations will be live by the start of May to allow Google sufficient time to crawl, index, and rank the updated pages before peak search volume hits.

Keyword scope

The strategy targets a mix of high-volume brand terms (Orphans In Need, orphansinneed), commercial intent Qurbani keywords, and informational Islamic content queries that build authority and drive organic traffic year-round.
