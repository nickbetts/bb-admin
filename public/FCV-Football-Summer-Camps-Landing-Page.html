Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Do players need a visa to attend? +
Players from outside the UK may require a Standard Visitor Visa. As this is a sports programme, most nationalities can apply for a 6-month visitor visa. We provide a full invitation letter to support your application at no extra cost.
What standard do players need to be? +
Players of all levels are welcome, from organised club football upwards. Our coaches assess each player on day one and tailor sessions to their level. You will be challenged and developed regardless of your starting point.
Can my child travel to the UK alone? +
Yes - many of our players travel unaccompanied from countries around the world. We offer airport meet-and-greet transfers and have 24/7 welfare staff on site throughout the entire camp. Parents receive regular updates.
What should players bring with them? +
Football boots (firm and soft ground), shin pads, casual wear, and personal toiletries. Training kits are provided. A full packing list is included in your camp welcome pack sent after booking.
Is there Wi-Fi and phone access at camp? +
Yes, Wi-Fi is available throughout Grace Dieu Manor Park. Players are encouraged to bring a mobile phone to stay in contact with family. We recommend downloading WhatsApp before travelling for easy communication.
What is NOT included in the £2,500 fee? +
Flights to and from the UK, travel insurance, and personal spending money are not included. Airport transfers from major UK airports are available as an optional add-on from £55. English language classes are also available at £200/week.
What happens after I submit the enquiry form? +
Our admissions team will contact you within one working day to confirm availability. To secure your place, a £200 deposit is required, with the full balance due 8 weeks before camp start. Deposits are refundable within 14 days of booking.
Is there specialist coaching for goalkeepers? +
Yes - we run dedicated GK sessions throughout the camp covering shot stopping, command of the penalty area, distribution, and footwork. Goalkeeper coaching is included in the standard camp fee at no extra cost.
