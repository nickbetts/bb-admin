# clickr by i3MEDIA

> clickr is an AI-powered landing page generator for marketing agencies. Paste a client's website URL, generate a fully branded, conversion-optimised post-click landing page, and publish it live in under 60 seconds.

clickr is part of the StratOS platform by i3MEDIA — a 16-channel marketing intelligence suite built for agencies managing performance campaigns at scale.

**What clickr does:**

- Scrapes any website URL to extract brand colours, typography, copy, services, testimonials, and team information
- Generates a complete, branded post-click landing page using that data via Meridian, i3MEDIA's proprietary AI
- Lets you refine the page via a chat editor — describe the change, and clickr rewrites it instantly
- Publishes to `{client}.clickr.marketing/{slug}` with lead capture, tracking pixels, and conversion notifications active from the moment it goes live
- Fires real-time alerts on conversion via Microsoft Teams, Slack, email, or any webhook

**Who it is for:**

clickr is designed for paid media teams, performance marketers, and agencies who need to ship high-quality post-click experiences fast — without a designer or developer bottleneck.

**Key differentiators:**

- Brand-accurate output from day one (real colours, real copy, real services — not placeholder text)
- Chat-based iteration — no form-filling or template-picking
- Clean subdomain publishing — `{client}.clickr.marketing/{slug}`
- CRM, Slack, Teams, and webhook integrations out of the box
- Part of the StratOS ecosystem, so campaign data, GA4, and ad performance can inform page strategy
